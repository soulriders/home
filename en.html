IP

Lambda IP lowers operating cost and makes the flight structure safer.

Receivepower IP should be read through structural change and operating effect, not just technical names.

Independent tilting thrust

A transition architecture that satisfies both site accessibility and fixed-wing cruise efficiency.

Virtual control surface

Mechanical control surfaces are removed. Differential thrust and aircraft geometry perform the control role.

Discrete control matrix

Flight modes are bounded, repeatable, and explainable through operating logs.

Passive high-lift and glide behavior

When thrust is lost, the aircraft is designed to transition into autorotation and glide modes for controllable descent.
